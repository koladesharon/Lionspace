Ofspace helped Lionstep’s to Design their Marketing Website with New brand.

Technology-driven sourcing platform Lionstep helps organizations find motivated, qualified, & interested candidates worldwide.
Through an active sourcing and reskilling program, LionStep connects talents with career advice and new career opportunities. In 2016, they started their journey from Zurich. Today, LionStep has a network of over 15 locations around the world and connects more than 40,000 people per month.
Our Service: Pitch deck design, Design system, Website & Landing page.

Challenge of work

Designing the website with a modern minimal design that offers users a seamless experience.

Research the competitive market for technology-based sourcing. Establishing a new brand that stands out from the previous one and competing in the market. Establish the new flow and define the structure which can be used iteratively.

Scope of work

Our team has completed competitor analysis, defined user flow, and most interestingly developed wireframes of various sections which will be used iteratively for the project. As well as developing visual designs for the web, mobile, and tablet with 100% component-based with auto layout.
